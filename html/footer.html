    Refund Policy

    We strive to ensure that every guest has a fantastic experience at our theme park.
    However, if you are not completely satisfied with your visit, please review our refund policy below:

    Eligibility for Refunds:
    Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the visit date.
    To be eligible for a refund, the request must be accompanied by the original receipt and a valid reason for dissatisfaction.

    Non-Refundable Items:
    Tickets purchased at a discount, annual passes, and special event tickets are non-refundable.
    Additionally, any used or partially used tickets cannot be refunded.

    Processing Refunds:
    Once your refund request is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.
